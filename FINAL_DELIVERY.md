### Common Issues

**Solution:** `pip install -r requirements.txt`

**Solution:** Delete `acetrade_research.db` (will recreate)

**Problem:** No data for ticker  
**Solution:** Verify format (SYMBOL.NS or SYMBOL.BO)

**Solution:** Normal, takes 30-60 seconds
